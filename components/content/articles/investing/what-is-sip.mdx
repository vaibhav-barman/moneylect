# What is SIP?

A **Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)** is one of the easiest ways to start investing.

---

## Why should students invest?

Starting early gives your money more time to grow through compounding.

---

## Example

If you invest **₹5,000/month**

for **20 years**

at **12%**

your investment can grow significantly because of compounding.

---

> Investing early is usually more powerful than investing large amounts later.
