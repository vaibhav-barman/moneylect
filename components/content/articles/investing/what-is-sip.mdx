# What is SIP?

A **Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)** allows you to invest a fixed amount every month into mutual funds.

<Callout>

The biggest advantage of SIP is consistency.

</Callout>

Instead of trying to time the market, you invest every month.

## Why students should start early

If you begin investing at age 21 instead of 31, the difference can be several lakhs because of compounding.

## Benefits

- Rupee Cost Averaging
- Compounding
- Discipline
- Low starting amount
